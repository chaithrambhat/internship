Blue Bird

The Ultramarine Flycatcher (Ficedula superciliaris) is a small, vibrant bird found in the Himalayas. Males are bright blue with white underparts, while females are grayish-blue. They inhabit forests, feed on insects, and migrate to lower altitudes in winter.

Dragon Fly

A dragonfly is a slender, agile insect with large eyes, transparent wings, and iridescent colors. Found near water, they are skilled flyers and predators of small insects.

Catabwa Falls

Catawba Falls is a scenic waterfall in Pisgah National Forest near Old Fort, North Carolina. Lower and Upper Catawba Falls. Perfect for moderate hiking and nature lovers
